Indian Ocean Trade
The Indian ocean had a very big impact. Here are some communities that left a lasting impact on other countries through the Indian Ocean Trade routes:
Arab and Persian communities
These communities were along the trade routes. Trade had brought religions such as Islam in these boats. Southeast Asia and Swahili’s city-states were affected.
Chinese communities
As trade progressed, China had spread Confucian values to Southeast Asia. These values included higher rights for women. They got jobs such as traders, artists, gladiators, poets, artists, religious teachers, and warriors in the Angkor kingdom.
Indian and South Asian communities
These communities actually impacted other communities. They had several people ruling the land, and had various languages such as sanskrit.
